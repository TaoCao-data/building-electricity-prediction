The Approach

The Data

Electricity Data

Year-long, hourly, metered electricity consumptions of ~1500 buildings in 8 typical weather regions in the USA.

Building Information

General information such as footprint, floor count and year built.

Weather Data

Weather data of 8 typcial regions.

The Benefits

Accurate

The tool has shown 10 to 50% prediction error reductions, compared to the widely used baseline approaches (average of similar past days). Learn more with the demo!

Flexible

The tool is flexible on what data your have (inputs can range from a few general building infomration to a complete hisotrical load profile) and what you want to predict (Learn more with the demo).

Easy to Deploy

Select from pre-trained models for quick tryouts, or train a dedicated LSTM model on Keras for your building with our pre-validated pipeline effortlessly.
